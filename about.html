Our Story

 
Mustard Grain is a development firm that was founded by Kirk True in 2005 to help companies scale their web applications to meet customer and data growth. We both use and contribute to open source projects that center around solving scalability concerns. We help startups and Fortune 100 companies (and the ones in the middle too) to create amazing web applications to achieve their business goals. And we love coming to work every day (except Friday).
